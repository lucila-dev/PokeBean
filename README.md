# PokeBean

Web app for keeping track of Pokémon TCG cards.

You can scan a card with your phone and it fills in the details (name, set, number, rarity, description). Or skip the camera and search the catalog — add cards straight from PokeWallet. Everything lands in your dashboard where you can filter, search, delete in bulk, and set your own price notes. Analytics page shows counts by set, year, and rarity.

Accounts are per-user — sign up, log in, your collection is yours.

**Stack:** Next.js 14, React, TypeScript, Tailwind, PostgreSQL + Prisma, NextAuth, OpenAI (vision for scans), PokeWallet API, Recharts.
